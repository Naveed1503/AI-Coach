Mastering Interview Etiquette

✅ 1. Entering the Room

Walk in confidently, with straight posture, a light smile, and a folder in hand. First impressions count.

✅ 2. Sitting Posture

Sit straight, no slouching. Keep feet flat and back aligned with the chair. Look alert and interested.

✅ 3. Upper Body & Hands

Keep hands visible and calm on your lap or desk. Avoid fidgeting. Relax your shoulders.

Interview Flow Visual

✅ 4. Gesturing Naturally

Use calm, expressive gestures when answering questions. Avoid excessive movement or stiffness.

✅ 5. Exiting the Room

Stand up confidently, shake hands gently, thank them sincerely, and exit gracefully with posture intact.
